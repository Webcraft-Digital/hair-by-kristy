About Me

Hi, I'm Kristy! I've been passionate about hairdressing since I qualified in 2009, bringing over 16 years of experience to every appointment. After 10 amazing years working in a salon, I took the leap to become a mobile hairdresser so I could offer my services in a way that's more personal, flexible, and family-friendly.

As a proud mum of four beautiful children and a happily married wife, I know just how busy life can get — and how important it is to still feel good about yourself. Whether it's a quick trim for a little one, a bold new colour, or styling for a special occasion, I love helping people feel confident and cared for through their hair.

When I'm not out on the road or chasing after my kids, you'll find me at Creative Heart Café in Littlehampton, where I take appointments two days a week in a relaxed and welcoming setting. I'm proud to serve the Angmering and Littlehampton areas with a friendly, professional touch — bringing salon-quality results straight to your door.
